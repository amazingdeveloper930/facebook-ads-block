How it works:

The Hack My Ads can only be used on Facebook. When you are on Facebook.com, open the extension and toggle the switch to activate the extension.

The extension will light up red when it is active and showing ads only. When it is inactive, it will be grey, as seen in the figure below.

    PRO TIPS
  • Scroll your feed for a few seconds before activating Hack My Ads for a smoother experience.
  • Refresh your tab once you scroll for a long time.
  • Open multiple Facebook tabs for more ads!
